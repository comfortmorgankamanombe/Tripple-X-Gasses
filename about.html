About Trippl Gases

Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2008, Trippl Gases has grown to become Zimbabwe's most trusted provider of professional gas installation, maintenance, and repair services. What began as a small team of passionate technicians has evolved into a comprehensive service provider with a reputation for excellence.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. Over the years, we've completed thousands of projects for both residential and commercial clients across Zimbabwe, earning numerous industry certifications and accolades along the way.
