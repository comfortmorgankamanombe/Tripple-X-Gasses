Gas Safety Tips

Important safety information every gas user should know

Install carbon monoxide detectors in your home and test them monthly. Gas leaks can be odorless and colorless, making detectors essential for early warning.

Leak Detection
Critical Safety Measure

If you smell gas (rotten egg odor), evacuate immediately. Don't turn lights on/off or use electrical devices. Call our emergency line from a safe location.
